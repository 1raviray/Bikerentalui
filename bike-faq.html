Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are needed to book a vehicle?

Aadhar card and Driving license You will need to share the documents before bike pick-up time

Is fuel cost included in the booking amount?

No, the fuel cost is not included in the vehicle booking amount. Fuel cost has to be borne by the customer.

Will I get a complimentary helmet?

One complimentary helmet will be provided to the customer at no extra cost. If the customer is accompanied by a pillion rider, we suggest the customer bring their own helmets.

What's the max distance (Kms per day) beyond which I should not travel in booked duration?

The Kms limit is different for each vehicle and can be seen below all vehicles listed on the vehicle selection page. If you ride the bike beyond that KM limit, additional charges of â‚¹3 / KM will be charged.

How much is the refundable deposit?

Refundable security deposit amount may vary based on the model of the vehicle. For any vehicle you select, the security deposit amount will be shown to you at the booking summary page.

Are Rental rides insured?

All rides booked through RaidPro rentals website are insured by RaidPro Rentals Protect. As part of RaidPro Rentals Protect we offer following insurance benefits:

  • Upto 3000 OPD claims in the event of an accident.
  • Rs 3 Lakh claim in the event of permanent disability to the Hirer.
  • Rs 5 lakh claim in the event of Death of the Hirer

Is it possible to extend the ride beyond booking duration?

Yes, booking extension is possible if the vendor accepts the booking extension request made by the customer.

What is the minimum and maximum rental period?

The minimum rental duration that can be selected on RaidPro Rental Platform is 3 hrs. You can book a bike for up to 2 months on the RaidPro Rental website, the longer the duration the more the discount you get. The discount is auto calculated and after applying the discount booking price is shown to you.

Is booking cancellation allowed? How much amount is deducted on booking cancellation?

Yes, customers can cancel the bookings from My Bookings section. Refund amount on cancellation depends on many factors like cancellation done by vendor or customer, acceptance status of booking request and cancellation time.

What's the Refund Policy on security deposits?

Security deposit should be given to the vendor at the time of bike pickup. It is 100% refundable on returning the bike. However, late fees or damage charges may be charged. You are advised to verify all such details with the vendor before collecting the bike.

I haven't received my security deposit back? Whom do I contact?

Security deposits should be collected from the vendor directly by the customer. The vendor details are shared at the time of booking confirmation. In case of issues, please write to us at support@RaidPro.rentals or reach out to us at 08061137444. Our team will address your payment/refund related concerns.

Are the bikes being shown owned or operated by RaidPro?

The bikes are neither operated or owned by RaidPro. RaidPro Rentals lists the bikes that are made available by our select vendor partners who own these bikes. All the bikes are owned and operated by these vendor partners.

I have a question that's not listed here, How do I contact the RaidPro Rental support team?

To contact, send a message at support@RaidPro.rentals or call 08061137444 Kindly mention your booking id if you are contacting us for an already existing booking.
