Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are needed to book a vehicle?

Aadhar card and Driving license You will need to share the documents before bike pick-up time

Is fuel cost included in the booking amount?

No, the fuel cost is not included in the vehicle booking amount. Fuel cost has to be borne by the customer.

Will I get a complimentary helmet?

One complimentary helmet will be provided to the customer at no extra cost. If the customer is accompanied by a pillion rider, we suggest the customer bring their own helmets.
